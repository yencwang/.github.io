About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Archaeology program at UCLA. My research focuses on the scientific analysis of organic materials, including animal and human remains. Topics I am interested in include using faunal data to understand biodiversity changes, and examining human skeletons to study patterns of care and resilience. I am also involved in historical archaeology in East and Southeast Asia, specifically examining the consumption patterns of trade ceramics across the seascapes.
